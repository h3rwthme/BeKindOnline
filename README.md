# Words Behind The Screen

**Words Behind The Screen** is a digital awareness campaign focused on preventing cyberbullying and promoting kindness in online spaces.

This project highlights how words typed behind a screen can affect someone's mental health, confidence, and sense of safety. Through visual campaign materials, posters, captions, and QR-based forms, this campaign encourages people to recognize cyberbullying, care for others, and report harmful behavior.

## Campaign Theme

**Cyberbullying Awareness Campaign**

Main message:

> Be kind online. Spread love, not hate.

## Objectives

* Raise awareness about the impact of cyberbullying.
* Encourage people to think before commenting or sharing.
* Promote empathy, respect, and digital responsibility.
* Provide a space for people to share opinions through a campaign form.
* Support the creation of a safer online environment.

## Campaign Message

Cyberbullying is not just a joke. Negative words, harmful comments, and online harassment can leave real emotional impact on others.

Behind every screen is a real person with real feelings.

## Key Points

### Recognize

Cyberbullying can happen to anyone, anytime, and anywhere online.

### Care

Support victims, listen to them, and do not join harmful conversations.

### Report

Report harmful content, save evidence, and use social media responsibly.

## Visual Concept

The poster uses a dark cyber-themed design with blue glow, glitch effects, floating chat bubbles, and blurred digital elements to represent the emotional pressure caused by online hate.
